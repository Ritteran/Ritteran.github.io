# Indian Income Tax Calculator FY 2024-25

A comprehensive, free, and open-source Indian Income Tax Calculator with support for all heads of income, depreciation calculation, and Old vs New regime comparison.

## Features

- **All 5 Heads of Income**: Salary, House Property, Business/Profession, Capital Gains, Other Sources
- **Depreciation Calculator**: WDV method with automatic calculation from purchase date
- **Presumptive Taxation**: Section 44AD, 44ADA, 44AE support
- **Weighted Deductions**: Scientific research, skill development as per IT Act
- **Regime Comparison**: Old vs New tax regime with recommendation
- **Special Tax Rates**: STCG @15%, LTCG @10%, Lottery @30%
- **Multiple Assessee Types**: Individual, HUF, Firm, LLP, Company

## Tax Slabs FY 2024-25

### New Regime (Default)
| Income | Rate |
|--------|------|
| Up to 3L | Nil |
| 3L - 7L | 5% |
| 7L - 10L | 10% |
| 10L - 12L | 15% |
| 12L - 15L | 20% |
| Above 15L | 30% |

### Old Regime
| Income | Rate |
|--------|------|
| Up to 2.5L | Nil |
| 2.5L - 5L | 5% |
| 5L - 10L | 20% |
| Above 10L | 30% |

## Usage

Simply open the calculator in your browser and enter your income details under each head.
